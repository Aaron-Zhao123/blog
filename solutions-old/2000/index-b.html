Solution notes in 2000

Access to this on-line version of the solution notes is restricted to the Examinations Officer and to Student Administration. Its sole purpose is to provide back-up versions for the Student Administration Office for when their paper copies go missing. This is Departmental policy, confirmed by the Teaching Committee (now the Tripos Management Committee).

Notes:

These solution notes were produced by question setters to assist the Examiners. They are not to be used as model answers, and it should be noted that different setters provide different amounts of information.

Some lecturers place solution notes on their own web pages. Where those exist, they should be used in preference to these versions, which may not be up to date (apart from Papers 1 and 2, they are as submitted to the Examiners in early 2000).

The files are in PDF format.
